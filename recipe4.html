Process

Preheat the oven to 175 °C - 195 °C.

First grate the zest from the 3 lemons, squeeze two of them.

Then beat the eggs and sugar until frothy. Sift the flour and gradually add it with the vanilla sugar, baking powder, lemon zest and margarine. Mix everything well. Spread the dough on a baking tray lined with baking paper. Place in the preheated oven and bake for approx. 20 minutes on the middle shelf.

Now gradually mix a glaze from the lemon juice and icing sugar - please use the lemon juice very sparingly, the glaze must be nice and thick.

While the cake is still warm, prick it all over with a fork. This will make it nice and moist, as it allows the glaze to soak in. Then quickly spread the glaze on the warm cake and let it cool.
